Kahaani Silk Care Guide

Gentle hands keep timeless stories alive. Each Kahaani scarf is crafted from pure China silk — a light, luxurious fabric that’s as delicate as it is enduring. With just a little care, your scarf will remain soft, radiant, and full of life for years to come.

When Your Scarf Arrives

  • Use a handheld steamer for a quick refresh.
  • If using an iron, select the lowest silk setting.
  • Or, simply let the scarf hang overnight to self-smooth.

Washing China Silk

  • Fill a basin with cool or lukewarm water.
  • Add a few drops of silk-specific detergent.
  • Submerge and softly swirl for no more than 3–5 minutes.
  • Rinse thoroughly with cool water. No twisting or wringing.

Drying Gracefully

  • Press the scarf gently between two clean towels.
  • Lay flat to dry away from any direct heat or sunlight.

What to Avoid

  • Bleach, fabric softeners, and harsh stain removers.
  • Perfume or hairspray applied directly on the silk.
  • Washing machines and tumble dryers.
  • Plastic bags for storage — silk needs to breathe.

A Kahaani scarf isn’t just worn — it’s lived in.
